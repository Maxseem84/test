IP Addresses (H4)

When you visit Cerri.com AG’s Websites, the Company collects your Internet Protocol (“IP”) addresses to track and aggregate non-personally identifiable information. For example, Cerri.com AG uses IP addresses to monitor the regions from which Customers and Visitors navigate the Company’s Websites.

Cerri.com AG could also collect IP addresses from Customers whey they log into the Service as part of the Company’s “Identity Confirmation” and “IP Range Restrictions” security features.

What is the payment plan? (H3)

We offer a flexible payment plan. When you upgrade from the free plan in Cerri, you will be billed monthly or annually depending on the plan you select. You can add and remove users at anytime. We accept all major credits cards via Stripe and Paypal, you may also pay by invoice. For more information Get In Touch or call us at 1-866-877-4364.

How can I assure IT that Cerri’s Cloud plan is secure? (H3)

From your account’s single page sign on onwards, Cerri uses advanced data encryption to ensure your account and data are protected from outside intrusion. (Your encrypted login data is transmitted via TSL (new SSL) or HTTPS port/443). Cerri runs on Microsoft Azure cloud, the award-winning cloud hosting provider. Microrsoft offers an uncompromising stability which safeguards from any inconsistencies or loss of data in case of system or power failures. We have several data centers around the world and comply with the European Union Security Standards; providing you with maximum data protection. More on security
